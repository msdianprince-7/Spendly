Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 8, 2026

1. Data We Collect

When you create a Spendly account, we collect your name, email address, and password. As you use the app, we also store the expense records you enter, including amounts, categories, dates, and descriptions.

2. How We Use Your Data

We use your account information to authenticate you and your expense data to generate the summaries, category breakdowns, and spending history displayed in the app. We do not use your data for advertising, and we do not sell your personal information to third parties.

3. Data Storage

Your account and expense data are stored in Spendly's database and retained for as long as your account remains active. You may request deletion of your account and associated data at any time, after which it will be permanently removed.

4. Third Party Services

Spendly does not share your expense data with third-party services. We may use third-party infrastructure providers (such as hosting or font delivery) solely to operate the app, and these providers do not have access to your personal expense records.

5. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or how your data is handled, please reach out to us through the contact details provided on your account page.
